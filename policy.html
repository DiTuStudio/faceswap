Privacy Policy

Last updated Mar 13, 2024.

Welcome to Morphify’s Privacy Policy!

If you decide not to read this entire Privacy Policy, we want you to walk away with a few key points about Morphify’s privacy practices:

  • The Apps are photo or video editors that allow users to swap portraits with highly realistic facial transformations.
  • We do not use photographs or videos you provide when you use the Apps for any reason other than to provide you with the swapping functionality of the Apps.
  • We use third-party cloud providers — specifically, Digital Ocean — to store photographs and videos.
  • You only send to the cloud the photographs or videos that you specifically select for swapping and editting.
  • Photographs or videos are temporarily cached on the cloud servers during the swapping process, and encrypted using a key stored locally on your mobile device.
  • Photographs may remain lifetime in the cloud, videos may remain in the cloud for a maximum limited period of 24 hours, for improved performance and lower bandwidth allowing additional changes to recently selected photos or videos in an optimal manner.

Here’s more on our privacy practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. When you use the App and select photographs or videos for swapping, we cache them for processing to render the requested swapping service:

  • Photographs or videos you select when you use the Apps, via your camera or camera roll (if you have granted the Apps the permission to access your camera or camera roll). We process only specific photos and videos you choose to modify using the Apps; and we do not collect your photo or video albums even if you grant us access to them. We safeguard each photograph or video that you select using an encryption key stored locally on your device. This means that the only device that can view the photo or video is the device from which the photograph or video was submitted using the Apps – the user’s device. Please note that while we do not require or request any metadata attached to the photographs or videos you select for swapping, metadata (including, for example, geotags) may be associated with your photographs or videos by default. We take steps to delete any metadata that may be associated with a photograph or video you provide when you use the Apps. Photographs may remain lifetime in the cloud, videos may remain in the cloud for a maximum limited period of 24 hours after your last swap or edit, so that you can return to it and make additional changes if you so choose.

1.2. When you use the App, we may collect some information, including:

  • App usage information and online activity data, such as information about how you use the App and interact with it, including the features you use, your preferred language, the date and time when you first installed the App and the date and time when you launch the app; Our service providers and certain third parties (e.g., online advertising networks and their clients) also may collect this type of information over time and across third-party websites and mobile applications. We may collect this information directly or through our use of third-party software development kits (“SDKs”). SDKs may enable third parties to collect information directly from the App.
  • Device data, such as your mobile device operating system type and version number, manufacturer and model, push tokens, Google Advertising ID and Apple ID for Advertising.

1.3. When you contact us by email, we may also collect:

  • Contact information, such as your name and email address, and information relating to your communication.

2.3. We will only process personal information on the basis of our legitimate interests where such interests are not overridden by your interests or your fundamental rights and freedoms.
